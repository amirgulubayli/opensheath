# Operating Rhythms and Ceremonies

## Sprint Cadence

- Sprint length: 2 weeks.
- Planning: first day of sprint.
- Demo + retro + go/no-go: last day of sprint.

## Weekly Operating Rhythm

- **Architecture Review (45 min):** decisions, ADR updates, dependency health.
- **Risk Review (30 min):** top 5 risks and mitigation progression.
- **Product Sync (45 min):** acceptance criteria quality and scope alignment.
- **Quality Review (30 min):** defect trends, flaky tests, release confidence.

## Daily Rhythm

- Standup: blockers, dependency calls, risk updates.
- Async status update by end of day with:
  - completed stories,
  - blocked stories and root cause,
  - plan for next working day.

## Decision Artifacts

- ADR for architecture/security/integration changes.
- Change request for any sprint scope delta.
- Incident ticket for production issues.

## Escalation Matrix

- **P0** security/data integrity issue: immediate incident response.
- **P1** sprint objective at risk: escalate to leads same day.
- **P2** non-blocking quality issue: triage in 24-48 hours.

## Delivery KPIs

- Planned vs delivered story points.
- Lead time from story start to merge.
- PR review turnaround.
- Escaped defect count per sprint.
- Mean time to detect and resolve incidents.
