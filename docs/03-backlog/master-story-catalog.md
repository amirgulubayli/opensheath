# Master Story Catalog

## Purpose

Canonical backlog list for engineering execution.  
Each story maps to a feature, epic, sprint target, and priority.

## Priority Scale

- `P0`: critical path or security/data safety.
- `P1`: high business value and key workflow completion.
- `P2`: important but can shift by one sprint.
- `P3`: optimization or post-MVP enhancement.
